Download & Setup

Download Butterknife for macOS

After downloading:

  1. Unzip the file.
  2. Drag Butterknife.app into your Applications folder.
  3. Right-click → Open (first launch only, macOS security prompt).
  4. Locate SuperSlicer when prompted.
  5. Start slicing your 3D models!

Butterknife

AI-assisted 3D Print Slicer for macOS and Ender 3

Please email me at jonathanyun617@gmail.com with questions and comments. Will be implementing most feedback, this is for you! Connect with me on LinkedIn.

Currently only supporting MacOS and the Ender 3/Neo series on a 0.4mm nozzle. It is relatively easy to expand to other systems so please let me know what would be useful.

Download & Setup

Download Butterknife for macOS

After downloading:

  1. Unzip the file.
  2. Drag Butterknife.app into your Applications folder.
  3. Download SuperSlicer and move it into your Applications folder. Ensure you can open SuperSlicer normally.
  4. Right-click Butterknife -> Open (first launch only, macOS security prompt).
  5. Locate SuperSlicer when prompted.
  6. Slice away!
